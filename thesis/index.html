The Graduate Exhibition Season 2014 presents work to the MICA community and the public from January 24 – May 4 and resumes June 26 – August 1. The season includes MFA, MA and post-baccalaureate exhibitions and critiques, gallery talks and presentations, public programs, a symposium, and student curated installations throughout the city.

The Graduate Exhibition Season showcases the achievements of MICA’s graduate students—a diverse group of artists, designers, educators, scholars, and curators. It offers the campus community and the general public opportunities to engage with and contribute to the creative process at MICA.

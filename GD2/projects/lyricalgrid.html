Lyrical Grid

Create a gridded HTML/CSS composition that uses rhythm, imagery, and symbolism to represent a song.

 

Step One

Pick a song that has lyrics

Step Two

Extract all the lyrics as text. Be sure to type them exactly as they appear in the song, ie. preserve repetition or word/syllable/line breaks.

Step Three

Analyze the rhythm and sequence of the lyrics. Is it even throughout? Are there more powerful moments? Does it build? Do any elements repeat? Is there a transformation?

Step Four

Sketch vertical compositions that visually represent your analysis and interpretation of the lyrics. What do lateral or vertical movement communicate? What do white space and gaps communicate? What does size communicate? What does density communicate?

Step Five

Create symbols, images and/or typography to represent your interpretation of the song lyrics.

Step Six

Translate sketches into a modular composition of squares that utilizes scale, form, and counter form to visually communicate your interpretation of the lyrics.

Step Seven

Using the provided code, combine modular structure, symbol, image, and text into a coded (HTML/CSS) map for your interpretation of the song lyrics.

Step Eight

Refine your lyrical map based on feedback from classmates.

Chicago Typographic Conference

The Chicago Typographic Conference is inspired by famous Chicago sculptures, such as the Cloud Gate and the Crown Fountain. These sculptures show great examples of glitch and distortion, which is what the visuals for this conference act as. This conference features numerous designers, which are shown in the Typographic Schedule, as well as promotional Instagram posts and a poster, all given to those attending the conference.

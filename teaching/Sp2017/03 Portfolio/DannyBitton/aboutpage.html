Hello! I am a freelance illustrator and comic artist from Miami, FL, and I love to depict places and tell stories through pictures. My work has appeared in a variety of formats including comics, book covers, picture books, and paintings. I am currently studying illustration at the Maryland Institute College of Art in Baltimore. For any and all inquiries, please contact me at dbitton@mica.edu
